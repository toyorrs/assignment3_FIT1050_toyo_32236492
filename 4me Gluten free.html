Our Promises

We prepare a menu that features over 100 gluten-free dishes, all of which are made from scratch and freshly prepared to order.

Rest assured, our products are made in a 100% gluten and nut free environment. We offer food alternative solutions to:

  • Food allergy sufferers(including Coeliac disease sufferers)
  • Nut allergy sufferers, lactose allergy sufferers
  • Customers who have specific dietary requirements
  • Organisations providing a nut free environment (including schools)
  • Health food stores and delishes
  • People that desire a healthier alternative

Frequently Asked Questions

At 4ME Gluten Free we believe that everyone should be able to manage their food allergies and intolerances. How? By offering a variety of 100% gluten free, nut free and dairy free products that don't just meet the needs of our food allergen / intolerant customers but tastes amazing to everyday consumers too!

Our products are made fresh, on site. So no, our products aren’t the kind that sit in the freezer for ages and then have that once-frozen-now-unfrozen taste (yuck!). Nor are our products full of preservatives like many gluten free products on the market. Our products are fresh, taste oh-so-good and are made from all natural ingredients.

Of course we are! We are certified and have won numerous awards as a result of our excellent service.
